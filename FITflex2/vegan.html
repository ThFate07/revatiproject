Vegan Diet Plans

Plan 1: High-Protein Vegan Diet

  • Breakfast: Tofu scramble with whole grain toast and avocado
  • Lunch: Lentil soup with quinoa and steamed vegetables
  • Dinner: Vegan chick'n parmesan with whole grain pasta and marinara sauce

Plan 2: Low-Carb Vegan Diet

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with almond milk, banana, and spinach
  • Lunch: Chilli Garlic Tofu
  • Dinner: Vegan stir-fry with tofu, mixed vegetables, and brown rice

Plan 3: Raw Vegan Diet

  • Breakfast: Fresh fruit salad with nuts and seeds
  • Lunch: Raw vegan wrap with avocado, sprouts, and mixed greens
  • Dinner: Raw vegan lasagna with zucchini noodles and marinara sauce
