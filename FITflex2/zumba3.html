Zumba Gold Plan
Zumba Gold is a Fantastic option for older adults looking to stay active.
WORKOUT SUMMARY
Main Goal Cardiovascular health
Workout Type Dance & Aerobics
Training Level Beginner
Program Duration 3 days
Days Per Week 3
Time Per Workout 60 minutes
Equipment Required Comfortable clothing, Footwear
Target Gender Male & Female
WEEKLY ZUMBA GOLD PLAN

Workout 1: WARM UP (5 to 10 mins )

Exercise Duration (mins) Rest
Gentle Marching 5 1-2 min
Arm Circles 5 1-2 min
Side Steps 5 1-2 min
Torso Twists 5 1-2 min

Workout 2:Main Zumba Gold Workout( 30 to 40 mins)

Exercise Duration (mins) Rest
Merengue March 10 1-2 min
Cumbia Side Step 10 1-2 min
Cha Cha Step 10 1-2 min
Salsa basic 10 1-2 min

Workout 3: Cool Down (5 to 10 min)

Exercise Duration (mins) Rest
Gentle Forward Bend 5 1-2 min
Side Stretches 5 1-2 min
Neck Rolls 5 1-2 min
Seated Stretch 5 1-2 min
