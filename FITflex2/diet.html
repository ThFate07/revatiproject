Basic Nutrition Guide

Nutrition is the process by which our bodies use food to grow, repair, and maintain themselves. A healthy diet provides the body with the necessary nutrients to function properly.

  • What is a Balanced Diet?

    A balanced diet is one that provides the body with the necessary nutrients, vitamins, and minerals to function properly. It includes a variety of foods from all food groups, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

  • Importance of Hydration

    Hydration is essential for the body to function properly. Water helps to regulate body temperature, transport nutrients and oxygen to cells, and remove waste products. Aim to drink at least 8 cups (64 ounces) of water per day.

  • How to Read Food Labels

    Reading food labels can be overwhelming, but it's an important step in making healthy choices. Look for labels that are low in added sugars, saturated fats, and sodium. Choose products that are high in fiber, vitamins, and minerals.
